Introducing MCoin, an ERC-20 token for use in the Chainz app. Buying a single token allows you to curate music within the app. More MCoin means more curation power!

What is Chainz?

Chainz is a music curation app focussed on discovering less popular songs and artists. Users who own at least one MCoin are allowed to curate any song they like. Even if you don't own the token, you are able to browse a fresh song playlist curated by users with the token.
